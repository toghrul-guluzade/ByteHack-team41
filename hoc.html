Our Hour of Code Project!

Our team participated in the global Hour of Code event at Baku Idrak Lyceum, aiming to make coding less intimidating and spark interest in computer science among about 20 students. We presented ADA University, emphasizing clear slides and engaging content. Students actively participated in coding exercises, showing progress and improved skills. The positive feedback highlights the event's role in breaking down barriers and fostering curiosity in the field of computer science.
